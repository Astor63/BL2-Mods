## *Stinger* Four-Walls Rebouncing

Increases by 5 times the number of ricochet from the Stinger's pistol fired bullets.

### Where to find it?

The *Stinger* is a Seraph pistol manufactured by *Vladof* and can only be obtained as a drop from the *Ancient Dragons of Destruction*.
